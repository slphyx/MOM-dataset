About

The MOM Dataset is designed as a benchmark resource for malaria microscopy research. The aim is to support reproducible development and evaluation of AI models for parasite detection, parasite counting, and stage classification.

Why this matters

Current malaria image research often lacks a standard dataset, standard annotation protocol, and standard evaluation method.

Initial focus

The first version is planned around Plasmodium falciparum thin blood smear microscopy images with instance-level annotations.

Intended use

The dataset can support benchmarking of AI approaches across parasite detection, parasite counting, and stage classification tasks.

Annotation structure

Each microscopy image may contain multiple parasite instances. Each parasite can be annotated with:

  • Bounding box
  • Stage label
  • Morphology-related features
  • Expert validation

Evaluation protocol

To reduce data leakage and improve reliability, the benchmark design should include:

  • Training, validation, and independent test sets
  • Slide-level or patient-level separation
  • Clear task definitions and reporting standards
